How to Use TigerThrift!

how to purchase an item how to sell an item
  1. Go to the buy page.
  2. Filter or use the sort button to go through items.
  3. Click on the details button if you want to learn more about a item.
  4. If you want to purchase a item, click the 'reserve' button.
  5. You've sucessfully reserved the item! Now, wait for the seller to reach out to you by email. They have 5 days to respond to your request.
  6. Go to your profile to see your reserved item. Here, you can see the time left for the buyer to reach out to you. If necessary, you also have the option to cancel a reservation.
  7. Once the seller reaches out to you, schedule a time to pay for and receive the item.
  8. Make sure to click the 'complete reservation' button after completing the transaction!
  1. Go to the sell page.
  2. Fill out the form with all the necessary details. For photos, you can upload up to 4 photos!
  3. If you want to purchase a item, click the 'reserve' button.
  4. Click 'Create Item!' to list the item. Your item should now be listed on the buy page.
  5. Once someone reserves your item, you will receive an email from us.
  6. The email will contain the contact information of the buyer so that you can get in contact with them. Schedule a time to pay for and receive the item.
  7. Make sure to click the 'complete reservation' button after completing the transaction!
