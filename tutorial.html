How to Use TigerThrift!

How to Sell a Product

1. Go to the buy page.
2. Filter or use the sort button to go through products.
3. Click on the details button if you want to learn more about a product.
4. If you want to purchase a product, click the 'reserve' button.
5. You've sucessfully reserved the item! Now, wait for the seller to reach out to you by email. They have 5 days to respond to your request.
6. Go to your profile to see your reserved item. Here, you can see the time left for the buyer to reach out to you. If necessary, you also have the option to cancel a reservation.
7. Once the seller reaches out to you, schedule a time to pay for and receive the product.
8. Make sure to click the 'complete reservation' button after completing the transaction!

How to Purchase a Product

1. Go to the sell page.
2. Fill out the form with all the necessary details. For photos, you can upload up to 4 photos!
3. Click 'Create Item!' to list the product. Your item should now be listed on the buy page.
4. Once someone reserves your product, you will receive an email from us.
5. The email will contain the contact information of the buyer so that you can get in contact with them. Schedule a time to pay for and receive the product.
6. Make sure to click the 'complete reservation' button after completing the transaction!
