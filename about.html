Thanks for using TigerThrift!

TigerThrift is a platform that helps Princeton students to buy or sell clothing.


Students can sell clothing by filling out the requirements on the sell page along with photo of the article of clothing. Students can purchase clothing by browsing the buy page. Our platform stream-lines the process of buying and selling used clothes on the Princeton campus.
